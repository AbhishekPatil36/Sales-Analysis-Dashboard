### 📊 Sales Analytics Dashboard

#### Transforming Sales Data into Actionable Business Insights

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and regional trends. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor key business metrics, identify growth opportunities, and make data-driven decisions through intuitive visualizations and dynamic filtering capabilities.


## 📌 Short Description / Purpose

The Sales Analytics Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of business performance by consolidating sales transactions, customer insights, product performance, and profitability metrics into a single interactive reporting solution. It helps business users quickly identify trends, monitor KPIs, and uncover actionable insights that support strategic decision-making.


## 🛠️ Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

• 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Primary platform for dashboard development and visualization.

• 📂 **Power Query** – Used for data extraction, cleaning, transformation, and preparation.

• 🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Developed calculated measures, KPIs, and dynamic business metrics.

• 🔗 **Data Modeling** – Established relationships between tables to support efficient analysis and cross-filtering.

• 📁 **Excel / CSV Dataset** – Source data for sales transactions and business reporting.

• 📄 **File Format** – `.pbix` for development and `.png` for dashboard snapshots.


## 📂 Data Source

**Source:** Sales Transaction Dataset

The dataset contains transactional sales records, including:

* Order Details
* Product Information
* Customer Data
* Sales Revenue
* Profit Metrics
* Regional Information
* Order Quantity
* Sales Dates

The data was transformed and modeled to support KPI tracking, trend analysis, customer segmentation, and profitability reporting.


## ✨ Features / Highlights

### 📈 Business Problem

Organizations generate vast amounts of sales data, but extracting meaningful insights from raw transactions can be challenging.

Key business questions include:

* Which products generate the highest revenue and profit?
* Which customers contribute most to overall sales?
* Which regions are performing best?
* How are sales and profits trending over time?
* What opportunities exist to improve profitability?

Without an analytical dashboard, answering these questions requires significant manual effort.


### 🎯 Goal of the Dashboard

The objective of this dashboard is to:

* Monitor key sales and profitability KPIs.
* Analyze customer and product performance.
* Identify sales trends and growth opportunities.
* Support strategic decision-making through interactive analytics.
* Provide stakeholders with a centralized business intelligence solution.
