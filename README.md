📊 Sales Analytics Dashboard | Power BI
🚀 Project Overview
The Sales Analytics Dashboard is a comprehensive Business Intelligence solution developed in Power BI to transform raw sales data into meaningful insights. This dashboard provides a 360° view of business performance by tracking revenue, profitability, customer behavior, and regional trends, empowering stakeholders to make data-driven decisions efficiently.

🎯 Business Problem
Businesses often struggle to extract actionable insights from large volumes of sales data. This project addresses that challenge by consolidating key sales metrics into an interactive dashboard, enabling users to monitor performance, identify growth opportunities, and optimize business strategies.

🔍 Key Objectives
✔ Monitor overall sales and profitability performance
✔ Analyze sales trends across time, regions, and product categories
✔ Identify top-performing products and customers
✔ Track business KPIs through interactive visualizations
✔ Enable data-driven decision-making through real-time insights
